About

I started experimenting with computer programming to varying degrees of success and with a variety of different languages and tools at the age of 12. I began by studying FreeBASIC, before moving on to HTML and CSS, before trying to jump into C++ and hitting a brick wall.
I continued honing my game development skills for many years after that before walking away from the craft during my later years of high school and my earliest adult years. I found my love for technology again around the age of 21 or 22 and got into studying computers on a much deeper level than I ever had before.
Though finding the free time to pursue all of my passion projects can always be a difficult thing, when I have the time I love to contribute to my projects and continously learn new technologies.
